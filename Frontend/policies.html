Glam Gait's Return Policy

Items purchased from Glam Gait can be returned within 30 days of delivery, except for certain items that are non-returnable. For details on non-returnable products, please refer to our Non-Returnable Items section.

If a specific type of product has a return period different from our standard 30-day policy, it will be mentioned under the product category. If no special mention is made, the 30-day return policy is in effect.

While we strive to match Glam Gait's return policy for convenience, please note that return policies from other sellers on our platform may differ. Products listed with Glam Gait Prime eligibility are subject to Glam Gait's return guidelines, which Glam Gait adheres to for consistency. For more on third-party seller returns, see our Third-Party Seller Return Policy.

Purchases made via the Glam Gait Global Store are subject to the Glam Gait Global Store Return Policy.

For details on how refunds are processed, including information on partial refunds and restocking fees, please see our Refunds section. If an item is mistakenly returned to us or an unintended item is included in a return, refer to our Mistaken Returns procedure.

Should you receive a refund but still need to return the item, you can initiate a return request through our Return Items You Ordered section. Please note, failure to return an item that is expected will result in charges.

If an item has been returned and you've been notified or charged, rest assured, we will reverse the charge once the return is processed. Be aware that your financial institution may need additional time to make the funds available in your account.

To ensure the safety and fairness of our return process, we may request additional information or documentation, such as a government-issued ID, under certain circumstances. This is part of our effort to prevent fraud and abuse. Any personal information provided will be treated in accordance with our Privacy Notice.
